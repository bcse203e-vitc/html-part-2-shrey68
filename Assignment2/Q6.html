Cake recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and sugar together.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and stir.
  5. Pour the mixture into a baking pan and bake for 30 minutes.
